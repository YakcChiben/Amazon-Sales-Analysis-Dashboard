# Amazon Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes a synthetic Amazon-style e-commerce dataset containing 100,000 transactions across 20 columns. The analysis focuses on revenue trends, top-performing brands and products, and regional sales distribution using **Power BI**.

## Dataset Details
- **Transactions:** 100,000 synthetic Amazon-style orders
- **Features:** 20 columns including customer, product, pricing, payment, logistics, and order status
- **Realistic Patterns:** Dynamic pricing, discounts, taxes, seasonal trends, payment diversity, and realistic geolocation

## Key Insights
- **Total Revenue:** $91.83M from 100K orders
- **Top City by Revenue:** Columbus ($4.62M)
- **Top Brand by Revenue:** CoreTech ($9.34M)
- **Top Product by Revenue:** Dress Shirt ($1.90M)

## Tools Used
- Power BI for data visualization and dashboard creation
- Data cleaning and transformation performed within Power BI

## Dashboard Views
1. **Revenue Overview** – Total revenue and orders
2. **Regional Breakdown** – Revenue by city and country
3. **Brand & Product Performance** – Top brands and products by revenue and quantity

## How to Use
Download the `.pbix` file and open in Power BI Desktop. Use filters to explore by region, brand, or time period.
